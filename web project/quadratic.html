Quadratic Formula Calculator

The quadratic formula is a fundamental tool for solving quadratic equations of the form ax2 + bx + c = 0. The formula is:

x = (-b ± √(b2 - 4ac)) / (2a)

Where:

  • x: This represents the solutions (roots) of the quadratic equation.
  • a: Coefficient of x2.
  • b: Coefficient of x.
  • c: Constant term.

The quadratic formula provides a method for finding the real or complex solutions to quadratic equations and is widely used in mathematics and physics.
