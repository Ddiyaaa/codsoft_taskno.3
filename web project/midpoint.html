Midpoint Calculator

The formula for calculating the midpoint (M) between two points, (x1, y1) and (x2, y2), is as follows:

M = ( (x1 + x2) / 2, (y1 + y2) / 2 )

Where:

  • M represents the midpoint, and it's a point with coordinates (Mx, My).
  • (x1, y1) are the coordinates of the first point.
  • (x2, y2) are the coordinates of the second point.

The formula averages the x and y coordinates separately to find the midpoint's position.
