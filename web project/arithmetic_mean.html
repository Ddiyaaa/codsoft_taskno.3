Arithmetic Mean Formula Description

The mean, often referred to as the "average," is a central statistical measure used to find the central or typical value of a dataset. It is a widely used measure of central tendency in statistics and is essential for summarizing and analyzing data.

Arithmetic Mean Formula

The mean (M) of a dataset x₁, x₂, x₃, ..., xₙ can be calculated using the following formula:

M = (x₁ + x₂ + x₃ + ... + xₙ) / n

Where:

  • M represents the mean (average) of the dataset.
  • x₁, x₂, x₃, ..., xₙ are the individual values in the dataset.
  • n is the total number of values in the dataset.
