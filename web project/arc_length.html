Arc Length Calculator

Arc Length = Radius × Central Angle in Radians

Where:

  • Radius (r): This is the distance from the center of a circle to a point on the circle.
  • Central Angle (θ in degrees): This is the angle formed by two radii of the circle, with the center of the circle as the vertex.
  • Formula Explanation: The formula for calculating the arc length involves multiplying the radius by the central angle in radians to find the length of the arc segment.
