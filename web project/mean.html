Mean Calculator

The formula for calculating the mean (μ) is as follows:

μ = ∑x / n

Where:

  • μ (mu): This represents the mean or average.
  • ∑x: Denotes the sum of all the values in the dataset.
  • n: Is the number of values in the dataset.

The mean is a measure of central tendency, representing the typical or average value in a dataset.
