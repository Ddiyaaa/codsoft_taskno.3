Vector Addition Calculator

In physics and mathematics, vector addition is the process of combining multiple vectors to determine the resultant vector. The formula for vector addition is:

R = A + B

Where:

  • R (Resultant Vector): This is the vector that represents the combination of vectors A and B.
  • A (Vector A): This is the first vector to be added.
  • B (Vector B): This is the second vector to be added.

Vector addition is used in various fields, including physics, engineering, and computer graphics, to calculate the net effect of multiple forces or motions.
